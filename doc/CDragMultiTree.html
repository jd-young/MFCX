CDragMultiTree

  • CTreeCtrl
    • CMultiTree
      • CDragMultiTree

CDragMultiTree is a drag 'n' drop supporting, multi-selection tree control.

Derive your tree class from CDragMultiTree. You may want to override the CopyBranch and DeleteBranch, and set the move, copy, and no-drop cursors by calling SetMoveCursor, SetCopyCursor, SetNoDropCursor (or SetCursors to do the lot).
